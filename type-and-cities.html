The Types and Cities poster series is a collection of posters that tells the story of Ho Thi Ky market through typography.

  • Typography
  • Visual Communication

Project info

Course: RMIT Type and Design
Duration: 1 month

Ho Thi Ky Market is a chaotic masterpiece. Its labyrinthine alleys and overwhelming stimuli create a sensory overload that is both thrilling and daunting.

A living, breathing entity, the market is a pulsating hub of human activity and desire. This poster series aims to capture the raw energy and intoxicating atmosphere of this extraordinary place, transforming its claustrophobic intensity into a visually captivating and inviting spectacle.
